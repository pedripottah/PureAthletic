# PureAthletic
PureAthletic is an in-development platform designed to help athletes of **all levels** train smarter through personalized recommendations, structured planning, and AI-powered insights. The project aims to bridge the gap between sports science and technology by providing athletes with practical tools to build effective routines, track progress, and make informed training decisions.

# Who is it for?
PureAthletic is built for anyone with an active lifestyle, with an initial focus on junior and amateur athletes. While professional-level features may be restricted as of now, the primary goal is to help everyday athletes train with greater structure, consistency, and a true purpose.

# Why PureAthletic?
Many people enjoy sports and exercise, but few have access to personalized guidance on how to train effectively. Beginners often rely on random workouts, inconsistent routines, or generic advice that doesn't match their goals or fitness level.

PureAthletic **aims** to change that by helping athletes understand what to train, when to train, how to recover, and how to progress through personalized recommendations based on their individual profile and activity.

# Planned Features
The following features are currently planned for future development:

Users can:
- Create a personalized athlete profile.
- Log daily training sessions across multiple sports (football, basketball, gym, running, and more).
- Estimate calories burned based on workout type, duration, and intensity.
- Set personalized goals based on available time, fitness level, and desired outcomes.
- Track training history and long-term performance trends.
- Keep all athletic data organized in one place.
- Receive AI-powered recommendations for training routines, recovery, and weekly planning.

# Vision
The long-term vision of PureAthletic is to become a platform that empowers athletes to train with intention rather than guesswork.

Whether someone is preparing for their first football tournament, improving overall fitness, or simply building healthier habits, PureAthletic aims to provide personalized guidance that helps users train more efficiently, recover better, and continually improve through data-driven insights and AI assistance.
